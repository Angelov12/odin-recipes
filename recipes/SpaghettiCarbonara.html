Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

An authentic Italian pasta dish using eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper. The secret is in the creamy emulsion created by the pasta water and eggs—no cream allowed!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Guanciale or Pancetta
  • Fresh eggs
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti until it is al dente.

  2. Fry the guanciale in a pan over medium heat until it is crispy and the fat has rendered.

  3. Whisk the eggs and grated cheese together in a small bowl until they form a thick paste.

  4. Reserve a cup of pasta water before draining the noodles.

  5. Toss the hot pasta into the pan with the meat, then remove from heat completely.

  6. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, adding splashes of pasta water and stirring rapidly to create a creamy sauce without scrambling the eggs.
