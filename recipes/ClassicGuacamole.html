Classic Guacamole

Description

The ultimate Mexican dip made with ripe avocados, fresh lime juice, and a kick of cilantro. It's creamy, zesty, and perfect for any gathering or snack time.

Ingredients

  • Ripe avocados
  • Red onion
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime juice
  • Jalapeño
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a medium bowl.

  2. Mash the avocado using a fork or potato masher until you reach your desired consistency (chunky or smooth).

  3. Chop the red onion, cilantro, and jalapeño very finely to ensure even distribution of flavor.

  4. Stir the chopped vegetables into the mashed avocado.

  5. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the mixture and season with salt and pepper.

  6. Mix gently and serve immediately with tortilla chips.
