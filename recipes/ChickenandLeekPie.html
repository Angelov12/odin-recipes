Chicken and Leek Pie

Description

Tender bites of chicken and slippery pieces of sweet leek smothered in a creamy white-wine thyme infused sauce, bubbling under a golden puff pastry crust. Chicken and Leek Pie is comfort food we dream about!

Ingredients

  • Leeks
  • Boneless chicken thights
  • Bacon
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Thyme and bay leaves

Steps

  1. Trim off the root and the dark green part of the leek. This part is reedy and not pleasant to eat. Discard or clean then add to your homemade vegetable stock.

  2. Cut the leek in half through the middle. Just to make it shorter which is easier to cut in half lengthways (next step).

  3. Cut the leek in half lengthways

  4. Separate the outer 5 layers or so from the core. If at this stage you notice dirt between the layers, give it a wash but try to keep the layers intact to make your cutting-life simpler / efficient.

  5. Cut outer layers into squares Cut the outer layers in half lengthways again, stack, then cut into 1.8 cm/0.75″ pieces to make square / rectangle pieces.

  6. Inner core Cut into 2cm / 0.8″ pieces.
